## PromptWars Challenge — Problem Paraphrase

The PromptWars Virtual Challenge asks participants to build an AI-powered solution that directly addresses the carbon footprint of AI workloads and digital services — a problem that is growing rapidly as organizations scale LLM usage across cloud regions without any visibility into the associated CO₂e emissions. Most teams and enterprises today cannot quantify the environmental cost of a single model inference call, let alone across multi-cloud, multi-modal, and multi-provider deployments. The challenge calls for a system that estimates, traces, explains, and helps reduce this hidden AI carbon footprint, while also surfacing Scope 3 indirect emissions linked to AI-enabled digital supply chains. Crucially, solutions must demonstrate Google Cloud-native architecture, agentic reasoning patterns, and actionable recommendations — not just dashboards.

## Solution Summary

VerdaTraceAI is a production-deployed, Google Cloud-native multi-agent carbon intelligence copilot that addresses every dimension of the PromptWars challenge in a single, cohesive system. It closes the AI workload carbon visibility gap by computing real-time CO₂e estimates via the formula `E = (P × I) / 1000` for any model, cloud region, and multimodal media combination, then surfacing those estimates through an interactive dashboard and what-if simulator. The solution extends beyond inference-time emissions to cover Scope 3 indirect emissions linked to AI-enabled services — including digital waste, food supply chains, and local commerce — through the LocalLoops module suite, rewarding sustainable behaviour with a carbon credits system. Built on Google ADK's Coordinator + Parallel agent patterns, Vertex AI Gemini 2.5 Flash, AlloyDB with pgvector, Cloud Run, and Firebase Hosting, the architecture achieves serverless-scale efficiency while maintaining an audit-grade, cryptographically verifiable carbon ledger for ESG compliance. Together, these capabilities position VerdaTraceAI as a complete, end-to-end answer to the challenge of making AI workloads carbon-transparent, Scope-3-aware, and continuously improvable.
